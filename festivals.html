Explore India's Vibrant Festivals

Celebrate the rich cultural diversity through India's most celebrated festivals.

Diwali

Known as the Festival of Lights, Diwali is celebrated with lamps, sweets, and fireworks.

Holi

The Festival of Colors, where people throw colored powder and celebrate with joy.

Navratri

A nine-night festival dedicated to Goddess Durga, filled with dance, fasting, and devotion.

Ganesh Chaturthi

A grand celebration of Lord Ganesha with idol processions and cultural events.

Lohri

A Punjabi festival marking the harvest season with bonfires, music, and dance.
